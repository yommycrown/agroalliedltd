About Us

Agro-allied Industrial Group

Zichis Agro Allied Industries Plc formerly Zichis farms limited is located at Imegun farm settlement along Ijebu Itele area of Ogun state . The farm is a mixed farming of cropping and livestock, strategically positioned as an Agro allied industries for backward and forward integration to address the food shortage in the nation and sub Sahara Africa

Zichis Agro Allied Industries Plc has grown asset base with Networth of over 450m and a share capital of 300m . ZAAI Plc recorded a whopping total gross revenue of =N=132 , 000,000 as at the close of business in December 2023 with profit after tax of over =N=8.1m with total retained earnings of over 21.1m

Our Livestock farm is siting on 18 Acres of land with over 10,000 growers and 25,000 layers for egg production.
We have also 22 earthing ponds stocked with over 2,000 catfish.
At ZAAI Plc . We equally breed snails and pigs for commercial purposes.

Palm Kernel Estate is sitting on 30 acres of land to our existing 5 acres with over 5,000 Ten-era palm fruits . In which will aid our oil production for local and export production .

ZAAI Plc has strategic alliance with local farmers to grow high breed maize and sweet corn which
has assisted us greatly in our backward integration towards feed mill production.
Our hydraulic feed milling plant has capacity to produce 10 -15 tons daily output which caters
for our animal feeds and at the same time we are set to deliver to other poultry farms within.
